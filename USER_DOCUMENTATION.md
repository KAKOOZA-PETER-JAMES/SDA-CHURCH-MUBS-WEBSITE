# User Documentation

## 1. Overview
Welcome to the SDA CHURCH MUBS system. This platform helps members register, receive updates, engage in the church forum, and access church content such as beliefs, events, and ministry resources.

## 2. Main Features
- Member registration
- Member profile badge in navbar after registration
- Forum/chat participation
- Events and ministry updates
- Our Beliefs and Our Journey pages
- Optional email updates for announcements

## 3. Getting Started
1. Open the website homepage.
2. Navigate using the top menu.
3. To join the database, open Register and complete the form.

## 4. Registration Guide
1. Go to Register.
2. Fill in required details:
- Full Name
- Email
- Phone
- Category and study/family details where required
3. Check the updates checkbox if you want update emails.
4. Submit registration and confirm.

Notes:
- If you register successfully, your name appears in the navbar badge.
- Your registered name is also used to auto-fill the forum name field.

## 5. Forum/Chat Guide
1. Open Forum from the quick actions or menu.
2. Enter or confirm your name in the Your name field.
3. Optionally set a topic.
4. Type and post your message.
5. Use available actions:
- Reply
- Edit (your own message)
- Delete (your own message)
- Reactions (Like, Amen, Pray, Love)

Attachments:
- You can upload files with your message.
- Recipients can open or download attachments.

## 6. Notifications and Updates
- Use the bell icon in the header to view updates.
- If you opted in during registration, update emails are sent to your email address.

## 7. Mobile Usage
- Open menu with the Menu button.
- Dropdown sections (Ministry, Media, Events, About) open on one tap.
- If the menu does not update immediately after a release, refresh your browser.

## 8. Troubleshooting for Users

### 8.1 I did not receive emails
- Check spam/junk folder.
- Confirm your email address is correct in your registration.
- Confirm you opted in for updates.

### 8.2 I cannot post in forum
- Ensure Your name is filled.
- Enter message text or attach a file.
- Refresh the page if connection status shows sync error.

### 8.3 Mobile layout looks wrong
- Refresh page.
- Clear browser cache.
- Update to the latest browser version.

## 9. Contact and Support
If you need help with registration data or access issues, contact church admin through official channels listed on the Contact page.
