At the heart of our mission in the Seventh-day Adventist Church – East-Central Africa Division is the belief that strong, healthy families are fundamental to building strong communities and nurturing faith. Ellen G. White, a foundational figure in our church, eloquently expressed the importance of family life, stating, “The happiness of families and individuals depends upon home influences. Eternal interests depend upon the proper discharge of everyday duties, the performance of which will bring peace and happiness.”

Pr. David Mbaga

Director

Pauline Kipkoech

Office Secretary

Our Commitment to Functional Families

We cherish the family unit as the cornerstone of society and uphold its pivotal role in fostering spiritual growth and personal development. Our commitment to supporting functional families is evident through various initiatives that promote:

  1. Family Unity and Togetherness: Encouraging families to spend quality time together, strengthening bonds through shared activities and meaningful conversations.

  2. Marriage Enrichment: Providing resources and programs that nurture marital relationships, offering guidance and support through workshops, retreats, and counseling services.

  3. Parenting Education: Equipping parents with tools and strategies to raise children in a nurturing environment grounded in Christian values, emphasizing love, discipline, and spiritual guidance.

  4. Crisis Intervention and Support: Offering compassionate care and practical assistance during times of family crisis or distress, ensuring families feel supported and valued.

Our Initiatives in Action

Through collaborative efforts with local churches, ministries, and community partners, we strive to create an environment where families can thrive spiritually, emotionally, and socially. Our initiatives include:

  • Family Life Seminars: Engaging workshops and seminars that address relevant issues facing families today, empowering them with knowledge and skills to navigate challenges.

  • Youth and Children’s Ministries: Providing age-appropriate programs and activities that instill Christian values and principles from an early age, fostering a foundation of faith within the family unit.

  • Community Outreach: Extending our mission beyond church walls to support families in need, offering practical assistance, and spreading hope through acts of service and compassion.

As we continue to uphold the principles of faith, love, and unity within our families, we invite you to explore additional information on the Adventist Family Ministries website.  Join us in building strong, resilient families that honor God and serve others.
